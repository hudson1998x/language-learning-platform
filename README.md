# LLE — Learn a Language by Living It

**Talk, translate, roleplay, and review — all in one place.**

LLE is a free, open-source language learning app built around one idea: you learn a language best by actually **using** it. Not by memorising word lists or filling in blanks.

Chat with an AI tutor. Translate your favourite songs. Practise ordering food or catching a taxi. Message characters who feel like real people. And when you come across something useful, save it as a flash card with one click.

---

## What You Can Do

### 🎵 Song Translation
Paste in any song and get every line translated with pronunciation and cultural context. No more guessing what the lyrics mean — you'll actually understand and be able to sing along.

### 🔄 Smart Flashcards
Every word or phrase you encounter anywhere in the app can become a flash card instantly. Review them in short study sessions that focus on what you keep getting wrong.

### 🎭 Real-Life Scenarios
Practise ordering at a restaurant, catching a taxi, checking into a hotel, and more. The AI guides you, corrects you, and explains the culture behind the words.

### 💬 AI Messenger
Chat with 16 unique AI characters — each one a native speaker with their own personality. Get corrected gently when you make mistakes, and turn every correction into a learning moment.

### 🤖 AI Tutor Chat
Your home base. Have real conversations with a patient AI tutor who translates, pronounces, and helps you save what you learn.

---

## Getting Started

1. Make sure [Ollama](https://ollama.com/) is running with the Qwen3 model.
2. Run `npm start` in the project folder.
3. Open your browser to `http://localhost:8080`.
4. Create an account (it's all local — just for keeping your progress separate from other learners).
5. Start learning.
