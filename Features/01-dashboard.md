# AI Tutor Chat

Your home base for language learning. Say hello, and a friendly AI tutor will chat with you in the language you're learning — correcting you, encouraging you, and keeping the conversation flowing.

![Dashboard](../Screenshots/dashboard.png)

---

## What You Can Do

- **Have real conversations** — type anything and the tutor responds naturally in your target language.
- **See instant translations** — every reply comes with an English translation and pronunciation guide.
- **Save what you learn** — one tap turns any tutor response into a flash card for later review.
- **Jump to any feature** — the dashboard is your launchpad for songs, scenarios, flashcards, and the AI messenger.

Think of it as a patient, personal tutor who's always ready to talk, never judges, and gives you all the help you need — translations, pronunciation, you name it.
